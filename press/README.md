## Guidelines for new releases

- **Filename:** `YYYY-MM-DD-short-kebab-slug.md`.
- **Front-matter:** include `date`, `author`, `repo`, `slug`, `licence` at the top.
- **Byline rule:** author is always the full formal — `Bryan Fred, Unitek Systems Limited, Bedford, United Kingdom`.
- **Dateline rule:** `**City, D MMMM YYYY**` (e.g. `**London, 11 May 2026**`). Full date, not just month.
- **Contact block at the end:**
  - Public discussion → `https://github.com/bryanunitek/TrueAI/discussions`
  - Private contact / connection request → `https://www.linkedin.com/in/bryan-fred-02209753/`
- **No personal email, no phone numbers, no Unitek generic inboxes** anywhere in the body.
- After adding a new release, add a row to the index above.
