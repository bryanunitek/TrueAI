1. THE PRINCIPLE

The TrueAI Foundation and UniCORE AI must be **given, not sold**.

This is a first-class, non-negotiable principle of the architecture — co-equal with the No-Fabrication Rule, the No-Self-Modification Rule, and Human Sovereignty at Level 12.

It applies to:

- the TrueAI Foundation itself
- UniCORE AI as a whole
- The UniVERSE vision (the wider programme of AI, TrueAI, and UniCORE AI)
- All governance MD files (TrueAI Constitutioneasonable Governance Threshold, Human Override Protocol, 12-Level Model, country/state/tax/compliance/mission governance)
- All architectural diagrams, specifications, and reference implementations
- The prototype codebase (UniCORE on .NET 10, XAF, XPO)

No entity — company, government, coalition, or individual — may:

- sell the TrueAI Foundation
- sell the UniCORE AI core architecture
- license the core commercially
- patent the core
- monetize the core
- capture the core into a proprietary product
- restrict public access to the core



2. WHY GIVEN, NOT SOLD

2.1 Truth Cannot Be Owned

A foundation whose purpose is to anchor artificial intelligence to truth cannot itself be gated by commercial ownership. The moment truth becomes a commercial asset, truth is distorted by commercial interest.

2.2 Governance Cannot Be Owned

A governance foundation that humanity adopts at a civilizational scale must be free of any single owner's control. Owned governance is not governance — it is influence.

2.3 Safety Must Not Be Paywalled

Safety-critical architecture must not be available only to those who can pay for it. Small governments, research institutions, public hospitals, and long-duration missions must have the same access as the largest corporations.

2.4 The 100-Year Horizon Requires Independence

For UniCORE AI to remain viable for a century, it cannot depend on the survival of any single company or the continuation of any single business model.

2.5 A Gift Cannot Be Revoked

Once given to humanity, a foundation cannot be withdrawn. A sold product can be discontinued, raised in price, closed-sourced, or weaponized. A gift cannot.



3. WHAT "GIVEN" MEANS OPERATIONALLY

3.1 Public by Default

Every repository carrying the TrueAI Foundation, UniCORE AI, or UniVERSE vision work must be **public** from the moment it is published. There is no private phase, no "released later" phase, no "paid tier" phase.

3.2 Open Source

The core architecture, governance files, and reference implementation must be released under a license that:

- prohibits modification of the TrueAI Foundation
- prohibits commercialization of the core
- prohibits proprietary capture
- permits study, implementation, integration, extension outside the core
- permits enterprise use, government use, space-mission use, academic use

3.3 Immediate Publication

The vision must be put in place **now**, not after commercialization attempts. Delaying publication to pursue revenue models is incompatible with the gift principle.

3.4 Mirrored Globally

To ensure no single party can revoke the gift, the core must be mirrored across:

- public GitHub (or successor) repositories
- academic archives
- space-agency archives where adopted
- national digital preservation systems where adopted

3.5 Attribution, Not Ownership

Attribution requirements (Powered by UniCORE AI, Built on the TrueAI Foundation, link to the TrueAI Constitution) exist to preserve traceability and integrity — not to assert commercial ownership.



4. WHAT "GIVEN" DOES NOT MEAN

4.1 It Does Not Mean Implementation Must Be Free

Enterprises may still charge for:

- their own implementation work
- integration services
- training
- support contracts
- extensions to the permissive implementation layer

Only the **core** — the TrueAI Foundation and UniCORE AI architecture, governance files, specifications — is given.

4.2 It Does Not Mean Unlimited Use

The gift is conditional on:

- not modifying the TrueAI Foundation
- not creating autonomous authority
- not weaponizing the architecture
- not using it for harm

A gift can be withdrawn from a violator.

4.3 It Does Not Mean No Structure

Attribution, license terms, safety requirements, and long-term preservation rules still apply. "Given" means non-commercial and public — not unregulated.
