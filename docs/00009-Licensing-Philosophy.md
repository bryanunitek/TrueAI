## 1. Purpose of this document

This document explains the licensing philosophy behind UniCORE AI, the TrueAI Foundation, and UniVERSE. It is companion material to the legal licence file in the repository root.

The binding legal licence is `LICENSE.md` in the repository root. That file is the operative legal instrument. This document explains the intent behind the choice.

## 2. The licence

UniCORE AI, the TrueAI Foundation, and UniVERSE are released under the **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International licence (CC BY 4.0)**.

CC BY 4.0 grants any person:

- The right to use the work, including commercially.
- The right to modify the work and create derivatives, including commercial derivatives.
- The right to redistribute the work and derivatives.
- A perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide grant of these rights.

The only requirements placed on users are:

- Preserve attribution to the original author.
- Indicate if changes were made.
- Provide a link to the licence.
- Do not represent the licensor as endorsing any particular use.

## 3. Why CC BY 4.0

The architecture is intended as a gift to humanity. A gift that restricts who can build with it is not a gift. CC BY 4.0 is the most permissive Creative Commons licence that preserves attribution. Attribution preservation is required so that the chain of authorship and the link to the governing documents (the TrueAI Foundation MD file, the UniCORE AI architecture, the UniVERSE programme) remains visible wherever the work is used.

This choice has structural consequences that the authors accept:

- Commercial use by any party is permitted. There is no permission gate. There is no commercial licence to negotiate.
- Closed-source derivatives are permitted. A user may build on this work and release their derivative under any compatible licence, including a more restrictive one. The original remains under CC BY 4.0 in perpetuity.
- Forks are permitted. Any fork that preserves attribution and links to the licence is licit.

These consequences are accepted because they are the price of a real gift. A licence that prevents commercial use is not a gift to producers building real systems in regulated sectors. A licence that prevents closed-source derivatives is not a gift to organisations that must protect specific operational details. A licence that prevents forks is not a gift to communities that need to adapt the architecture to local contexts.

## 4. What CC BY 4.0 does not protect

CC BY 4.0 is a copyright licence. It governs the use of the textual and structural content of the work. It does not govern:

- The **names** "UniVERSE", "TrueAI", "UniCORE AI", "TrueAI Foundation". These are protected by trademark.
- The **claim of endorsement** by Unitek Systems Limited, the TrueAI Foundation, or the UniCORE Foundation. Use of these endorsements is governed by the Brand and Trademark Use Policy.
- The **attribution chain**. False attribution, removal of attribution, or misrepresentation of authorship is governed by general law (passing-off, misrepresentation) in addition to the licence's attribution requirement.

For details of the brand and trademark protections, see `BRAND-AND-TRADEMARK-USE-POLICY.md`.

## 5. Permitted uses, with examples

Under CC BY 4.0, all of the following are permitted:

- A bank builds a governance system on the 12-Level UniCORE AI model and integrates it into a paid product. Permitted.
- A research group forks UniCORE AI, modifies it for an academic experiment, and publishes the modified version under a different open licence. Permitted.
- A government agency integrates the architecture into a sovereign compliance system. Permitted.
- A consultancy offers paid services to implement the architecture for clients. Permitted.
- A vendor includes the architecture inside a closed proprietary product. Permitted, provided attribution is preserved.

In each case the only constraints are: attribution preservation, indication of changes, link to the licence, no claim of endorsement.

## 6. What is asked of users (not legally binding, but requested)

The authors ask, as a matter of principle rather than legal restriction, that users of this work:

- Preserve human sovereignty in any implementation.
- Honour the Human Override Protocol and the Reasonable Governance Threshold.
- Refrain from building systems that are self-modifying or that claim autonomous authority.
- Refrain from using the architecture to weaponise AI decision-making against humans.

These requests are guidance, not legal restrictions. The licence does not enforce them. Users who depart from this guidance are not in breach of the licence; they are simply not aligned with the authors' intent. The authors retain the right to publicly note such departures and to decline endorsement.

## 7. Long-term preservation

The licence is irrevocable. Once granted under CC BY 4.0, the right to use the work persists in perpetuity for all recipients. Anyone who downloads or receives this work retains those rights forever, even if future versions of the work are released under different terms.

This irrevocability is intentional. It guarantees that the gift cannot be withdrawn by a future custodian, a successor entity, or a hostile actor. The architecture remains available to humanity for as long as any copy of the work exists.

To support long-term preservation in practice, the authors maintain:

- The canonical repositories on GitHub: the **Foundation triad** — `bryanunitek/UniVERSE`, `bryanunitek/TrueAI`, `bryanunitek/UniCORE-AI` — and the **gift-layer extension** — `bryanunitek/UniCORE`, `bryanunitek/UniCORE.GVB`.
- Active encouragement of independent mirrors and archives.
- A clear chain of attribution that ties any compliant implementation back to the canonical source.

## 8. Role of Unitek Systems Limited

Unitek Systems Limited is the originator of the work and the present custodian of the brand. Its role is:

- To maintain the canonical repositories.
- To register and enforce trademarks on the programme names.
- To publish and maintain the Brand and Trademark Use Policy.
- To respond to misuse, passing-off, and false-endorsement claims through trademark enforcement and general law, not through the copyright licence.

The licence is not enforced by Unitek Systems Limited restricting its terms. The licence is protected by Unitek Systems Limited enforcing the surrounding trademark and brand-use framework, leaving the licence itself maximally permissive.

## 9. Role of the Foundations

The TrueAI Foundation and the UniCORE Foundation, once constituted, will sit independently of Unitek Systems Limited. Their role is to oversee the wider ecosystem of producers building on the work. They are not commercial licensing bodies. They do not issue commercial licences and do not collect commercial licensing fees. The work is already commercially usable under CC BY 4.0 without their permission.

## 10. Summary

The licence is CC BY 4.0. The licence is a real gift. The licence is irrevocable. The brand and trademarks are separately protected. The Foundations oversee the ecosystem, not the licence.
