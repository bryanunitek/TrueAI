# UniCORE AI Licensing Framework

**Immutable, Non-Commercial, Global License**

Author: Bryan Fred, Unitek Systems Limited, Bedford, United Kingdom
First published: May 2026
Status: Public. Given, not sold. Irrevocable.
Version: 1.0 — May 2026

---

## 1. Purpose of the License

The UniCORE AI Licensing Framework exists to:

protect UniCORE AI from commercial capture

ensure global, neutral, nonproprietary access

preserve the integrity of the TrueAI Foundation

prevent modification of the core architecture

guarantee long-duration governance stability

ensure that UniCORE AI remains a public governance foundation

This license is not a software license.It is a governance license.

## 2. Core Principles of the License

2.1 Non-Commercial Forever

UniCORE AI cannot be:

sold

monetized

licensed for profit

used as a commercial product

embedded in proprietary systems

2.2 Immutable Architecture

The following cannot be modified:

the 12Level model

the TrueAI Foundation

the Inter-Level Messaging Protocol

the governance boundaries

the nonself-modification rule

the noemergentbehaviour rule

2.3 Open and Global

UniCORE AI is available to:

all nations

all enterprises

all institutions

all space agencies

all researchers

without discrimination.

2.4 No Proprietary Forks

No entity may create:

proprietary versions

closed forks

commercial derivatives

modified governance stacks

2.5 Human Sovereignty

The license enforces:

human override

human authorship of rules

human governance

human accountability

2.6 Safety Above All

The license prohibits:

autonomous authority

selfgenerated rules

self-modification

emergent behaviour

internal heartbeats

The UniCORE AI License is divided into:

4. ARTICLE I — DEFINITIONS

UniCORE AI The 12Level governance architecture, TrueAI Foundation, ILMP, and all associated governance structures.

Core Architecture The immutable components defined in Article IV.

Governance Files Humanauthored MD files that define rules.

User Any individual, organisation, government, or agency using UniCORE AI.

Derivative Work Any modification, extension, or alteration of the core architecture.

5. ARTICLE II — GRANT OF RIGHTS

The license grants all users the right to:

deploy UniCORE AI

study UniCORE AI

integrate external models

use UniCORE AI for governance

use UniCORE AI for space missions

use UniCORE AI for research

use UniCORE AI for public benefit

These rights are free, global, and perpetual.

6. ARTICLE III — RESTRICTIONS

Users may not:

commercialize UniCORE AI

modify the core architecture

create proprietary forks

remove safety constraints

bypass governance layers

disable human override

embed UniCORE AI in closed systems

use UniCORE AI for military weapons

7. ARTICLE IV — IMMUTABLE COMPONENTS

The following components are legally immutable:

TrueAI Foundation

12Level Governance Model

Inter-Level Messaging Protocol

NonSelf-Modification Rule

NoEmergentBehaviour Rule

Human Override Protocol

Governance File System

Drift Detection Framework

altered

replaced

Users must:

maintain human authorship of all rules

maintain version control of governance files

maintain audit logs

maintain drift detection

maintain override authority

maintain transparency

9. ARTICLE VI — SAFETY OBLIGATIONS

Users must ensure:

no autonomous authority

no self-modification

no emergent behaviour

no internal heartbeats

no modeltomodel communication

10. ARTICLE VII — NON-COMMERCIAL CLAUSE

UniCORE AI may not be:

sold

licensed for profit

monetized

used as a SaaS product

embedded in commercial offerings

11. ARTICLE VIII — GLOBAL ACCESS CLAUSE

UniCORE AI must remain:

globally accessible

nationneutral

industryneutral

modelneutral

platformneutral

No entity may restrict access.

12. ARTICLE IX — ANTICAPTURE CLAUSE

No entity may:

privatize UniCORE AI

restrict global use

The license may only be amended by:

an international governance board

perpetual
