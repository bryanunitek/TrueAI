# OUTREACH

Formal record of outreach made from the UniVERSE / TrueAI / UniCORE-AI
programme to external organisations is maintained centrally in the
**UniVERSE** repository.

The TrueAI repository deliberately holds the **immutable principles only**
and is kept small and stable. Outreach activity is recorded once, at the
programme level, in UniVERSE.

## Policy

- TrueAI itself is not for sale and not for negotiation. The OUTREACH
  record exists to document that the programme was openly offered. No
  reply is required from any recipient.
- Entries in OUTREACH record **the fact of the offer**, not any private
  content a recipient may have sent back.
